About me
My name is Andre, I am a professional software engineer and entrepreneur.

Some of my biggest interests are Flutter, Low Level Systems, Compilers, Hardware, Esolangs, Backend Systems, Networking, and Robotics.

From 2012 to 2017 I competed in VEX Robotics where I was a lead programmer and designer, within those 6 years my robotics team won over 30 awards including finalist at the U.S. national robotics tournament.

In 2017 I co-founded Realism, a startup that set out to revolutionize modern science education by making hands-on virtual lab simulations accessible to all. We later sold Realism to another edtech company.

In 2021 I co-founded Dub and am the current CTO, a stock trading app that also lets you invest in user-made portfolios. Since then we have grown to ~20 employees and are an SEC-approved RIA (Registered Investment Advisor).
