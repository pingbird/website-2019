About me
My name is ping, I am a professional software engineer and entrepreneur.

Some of my biggest interests are Flutter, Cloud Architecture, Low Level Systems, Security, Compilers, Hardware, and Robotics.

From 2012 to 2017 I competed in VEX Robotics where I was a lead programmer and designer, within those 6 years my robotics team won over 30 awards including finalist at the U.S. national robotics tournament.

In 2017 I co-founded Realism, a startup that set out to revolutionize modern science education by making hands-on virtual lab simulations accessible to all. We later sold Realism to another edtech company.

In 2021 I co-founded Dub, the first stock trading app that lets you invest in user-made portfolios. Since then, we've grown to around 20 employees and are an SEC-approved RIA (Registered Investment Advisor).
