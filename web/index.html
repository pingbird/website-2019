About me
My name is Andre, I am a professional software developer and graphic designer.

I have a passion for everything related to computers and focus on making things that are both novel and difficult to build, everything from compilers to websites to hardware.

In 2017 I co-founded Realism, a startup that set out to revolutionize modern science education by making hands-on virtual lab simulations accessible to all.

From 2012 to 2017 I competed in VEX Robotics where I was a lead programmer and designer, within those 6 years my robotics team won over 30 awards including finalist at the U.S. national robotics tournament.

In 2015 I started Community Robotics of Metro Detroit, a 501(c)(3) non-profit dedicated to inspire disadvantaged K-12 youth to become engineers.
